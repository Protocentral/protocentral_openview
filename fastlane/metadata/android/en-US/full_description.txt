OpenView is the companion app for ProtoCentral biosignal and sensor boards. It
connects to a board over Bluetooth Low Energy or Wi-Fi, decodes the incoming
data into signals, plots it live, and records it for later replay and analysis.

One app works with every supported ProtoCentral board, including the HealthyPi
family and the various ADS, MAX and AFE sensor breakouts.

Features:

* Real-time plotting of ECG, respiration/bio-impedance, PPG and other signals
* Connect over Bluetooth Low Energy or Wi-Fi (USB is desktop-only)
* Record sessions to file and replay them later
* Export recorded data to CSV
* Adapts from a phone bottom bar to a tablet navigation rail

OpenView is free and open source software (MIT licensed). It contains no ads,
no analytics and no tracking.

Note: this build talks to hardware. You need a compatible ProtoCentral board to
stream live data. See the project page for the list of supported boards and
build documentation.
